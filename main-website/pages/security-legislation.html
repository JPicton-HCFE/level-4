Cybersecurity Legislation

Overview

On this page I will be talking about the Cybersecurity legislations, regulations, and standards.

Legislations

  • DPA (Data Protection Act) & (GDPR) General Data Protection Regulation
  • CMA (Computer Misuse Act)
  • CDPA (Computer, Designs and Patents Act
  • ISA (Intelligence Services Act)
  • RIPA (Regulation of Investigatory Powers Act)
  • ISO (Information Security Management Systems)
  • NCSC (National Cyber Security Centre)
  • NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology)
